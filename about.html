About Me

It's nice to meet you!

Hey! I'm Haroon. I'm an interdisciplinary designer with a background in industrial design, UX, and mathematics. I research, design, build, and sometimes code. I love technically challenging problems, and I hope to use my skills to help make the world a more honest, accessible, and sustainable place.

In my work, I hope to utilize my creative skillset to create novel digital experiences that help users and businesses solve meaningful problems in the world. In my free time, I am always seeking to expand my abilities both physically and digitally. As a curious maker I spend most of my down time exploring new facets of art & design that allow me express myself and the ideas I wish to share with the world. However, when not glued to a screen, you can probably find me exploring new trails around me!

Though I began my undergraduate journey studying mathematics, I quickly realized I had more to offer the world. I chose to embark on a new path, and recently received my Bachelor's in Industrial design (B.S.) from James Madison University. As the son of two architects, I learned being a designer is not merely a career, but a life. I hope to spend the rest of mine positivley effecting the digital ecosystem we live in. Lets chat!
