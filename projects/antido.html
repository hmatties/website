Context

Antido is a social media platform designed to help users build and maintain meaningful social connections.

The app blends the therapeutic benefits of journaling with the connectivity and design patterns of traditional social platforms to create a novel, unique communication experience.

Problem

Our current digital connections are plagued by deceit, showmanship, and misrepresentation.

The current ecosystem of social platforms have failed us, and their ever-present drawbacks have begun unravel the fabric of our societies. These drawbacks have contributed to the alarming uptick in the prevalence of major depression and suicide in adolescent groups, the growing dependency on connection, and the volatile role technology has played in our political and media systems.

This drastic problem facing us pushed me to consider how we might rethink our modern digital connections for the better.

The design process began by establishing a clear and focussed feature set to build around. I then highlighted key areas in which interactions required more context and detail. Lastly, I wireframed and visualized key screens and flows to communicate all the unique offerings Antido brings.

Information Architecture

I utilized broad information architecture flows to better understand how loose feature sets might fit together in a cohesive application. This style of hierarchy diagram allowed me to blend both overarching application structure with the content utilized within it.

Upon identifying a clear structure and highlighting core feature sets, user flows were then utilized to solidify the reach and functionality of certain features. This process allowed me to refine application wide functionality, while expanding upon specific utilities.

Communication

Antido provides users a platform to communicate honestly. It‚Äôs novel communication methods allow users to be open with themselves and their friends. It blends aspects of journaling with standard digital communication patterns to provide a fresh view of what modern digital interactions can look like.

Cards

Antido allows users to post and connect with others through the creation of unique multimedia cards.

These cards can be guided by thought-provoking prompts or be built from scratch. The combination of photo/video, voice, text, embedded music, etc.. allows users to express themselves in more nuanced ways.

Connections

Antido facilitates the creation of smaller, personal social circles. Antido removes the follower-dynamic with a simple, and equal, two-way connection. Antido pushes users to make these connections where they started, in-person.

To make a connection on Antido, you have to have a social connection outside of the app as well. Users can primarily make a connection through their own unique QR-codes or links, making connections seamless.

Takeaways

Great designers are genre-less

Antido was my first exploration into the world of digital product design. I worried how my skills would and wouldn‚Äôt transfer, but this project taught me that medium is meaningless. My value and skills as a designer lie in my ability to understand, synthesize, ideate, and implement. The tools used to do this are, and will continue to be, flexible. I learned removing the genres and expectations of output provided me the oppurtunity to do what I as a designer should be doing, solving a problem.

Restriction has value

When evaluating competitors in this market I noticed an alarming trend of companies and platforms each seeking to build products that envelop all communication. I beleive this panoptic view of their roles in the world strip the product of its novelty and value. Though it may provide flexibility to the user, I believe the lack of focus can be very overwhelming for users at times. This insight really showed me the value in finding your lane, and staying in it.

Build a tool kit

A major focus for this project was to communicate the visual motifs and identity of the product through animation. This facet of Antido really highlighted the value tools like animation, and design tools in general, truly have. I learned all these tools are simply modes of communication, and their amalgamation and composition work to paint a cohesive picture of the idea you are seeking to communicate. This insight really changed the way I looked at my design education, holistically. I began to see the skills I was building as tools within my ‚Äòtool kit‚Äô. Regardless of the project or medium, I have a slew of tools at my disposal that I can utilize the effectively communicate my ideas, and I should never assume what may or may not be necessary to effectively communicate my ideas.
